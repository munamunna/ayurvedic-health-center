Holistic and Quality Ayurvedic Care in Kerala

At Dr. Acharya Ayurveda Clinic, our vision is to provide state-of-the-art treatment and care without compromising on quality. We meticulously evaluate each patient through detailed consultations, crafting personalized treatment plans that incorporate herbs and traditional Ayurveda therapies.

Our therapies are designed to restore balance and harmony within the body, mind, and spirit. Whether you're seeking preventative care or specialized treatments, we provide a holistic approach to healing that is rooted in Ayurvedic principles.

Our Ayurvedic Treatments

Abhyanga

A full-body oil massage that rejuvenates the body and mind, promoting relaxation and overall well-being.

Shirodhara

A soothing treatment involving a continuous flow of warm oil on the forehead, calming the nervous system.

Panchakarma

A detoxification process that eliminates toxins from the body through various therapeutic treatments.

Nasya

A nasal therapy that clears sinus passages and promotes respiratory health using herbal oils and powders.

Kati Basti

A localized treatment for lower back pain using warm herbal oils held in a dough dam on the back.

Udwarthanam

A vigorous herbal powder massage that helps in weight management and improves circulation.
